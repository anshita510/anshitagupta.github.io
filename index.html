Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur under the esteemed guidance of Prof. Sudip Misra. My research focuses on collaborative edge intelligence for IoT healthcare, including federated learning, on-device machine learning, and causal analysis of health events.

I work on building practical systems that use smartphones, wearables, and other resource-constrained devices for health monitoring, ranging from lung health screening tools to stress and well-being applications for students. I have a vision of making AI-driven healthcare support more accessible and usable in everyday settings.

I also teach and mentor interns and students at IIT Kharagpur as a CEP lecturer and teaching assistant for core computer science and emerging technology courses. My long-term goal is to design deployment-ready AI systems that fit naturally into real-world healthcare systems.
